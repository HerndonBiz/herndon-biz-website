Data & Reporting

How I Track What Works

Every client gets Google Analytics 4 and Search Console set up on day one. Every month, you get a plain-English report. Real data. Real growth. No dashboards to figure out.

The Tools

Two free tools. Powerful together.

Google Analytics 4 and Search Console are industry-standard. I set them up, read them, and translate them into plain English for you — every single month.

Google Analytics 4

GA4 shows you everything happening on your website — who's visiting, where they came from, and what they do once they arrive.

  • Total visitors and unique visitors per month
  • Where traffic comes from (Google, social, direct, referrals)
  • Which pages get the most attention
  • Phone number clicks tracked as conversions
  • Contact form submissions tracked as conversions
  • How long people stay and what they read
  • Mobile vs. desktop breakdown
  • Geographic location of visitors

Google Search Console

Search Console shows you exactly how Google sees your site and what searches are driving people to find you.

  • What keywords people type to find your business
  • How often your site appears in search results
  • Click-through rates from Google to your site
  • Your average search ranking position
  • Which pages rank for which terms
  • Indexing status — is Google finding all your pages?
  • Mobile usability issues flagged immediately
  • Month-over-month ranking improvements
