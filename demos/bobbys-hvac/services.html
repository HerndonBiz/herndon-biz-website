Service 01

HVAC Installation & Replacement

When your system is beyond repair or simply too old to run efficiently, Bobby's HVAC delivers a seamless replacement that pays for itself in energy savings. We assess your home's square footage, insulation, and duct condition to recommend the right-sized unit — no overselling, no shortcuts.

We install Carrier, Trane, Lennox, and Rheem systems with full manufacturer warranties and our own 1-year labor guarantee. Most residential replacements are completed in a single day.

  • Free in-home load calculation and equipment recommendation
  • Full removal and disposal of old equipment
  • Installation of new indoor and outdoor units
  • Refrigerant charge, system startup, and performance verification
  • Thermostat installation or smart thermostat upgrade
  • 1-year labor warranty + full manufacturer equipment warranty

Service 02

HVAC Repair & Maintenance

Most HVAC problems are caught early — and fixed cheaply — with an annual tune-up. Bobby's maintenance visits are thorough: we clean coils, check refrigerant pressure, test capacitors, inspect heat exchangers, and verify airflow throughout your home.

When something does break, our trucks are stocked with the most common parts so most repairs are done in a single visit. We diagnose before we recommend — you'll know exactly what's wrong and what it costs before we touch a thing.

  • Same-day and next-day repair appointments available
  • Full 21-point seasonal tune-up (heating and cooling)
  • Refrigerant leak detection and recharge
  • Capacitor, contactor, and motor replacement
  • Blower motor, evaporator coil, and heat exchanger inspection
  • Annual maintenance plans with priority scheduling

Service 03

Indoor Air Quality

The air inside your home can be 2–5x more polluted than outdoor air. Dust, pollen, mold spores, VOCs, and bacteria circulate through your HVAC system every day. Bobby's installs proven solutions that make a real difference — especially for families with allergies, asthma, or young children.

We assess your current air quality and recommend targeted solutions. No upselling — just the equipment that solves your actual problem.

  • Whole-home air purifiers and UV germicidal lights
  • HEPA and media filter upgrades
  • Whole-home humidifiers and dehumidifiers
  • Duct cleaning and sanitizing
  • Ventilation and fresh air intake systems
  • Air quality testing and written report
