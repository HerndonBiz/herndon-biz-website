WHY CHARLOTTE HOMEOWNERS TRUST US

Bobby's HVAC was founded on one principle: do the job right and treat people fairly. Bobby Hernandez started the company in his truck with a single set of tools and a reputation for showing up when others didn't. Twenty years later, the company has grown into one of Charlotte's most-referred HVAC contractors — but the work ethic hasn't changed.

We don't upsell systems you don't need. We'll tell you honestly whether your unit needs a repair or a replacement, and we'll back our recommendation with real data — efficiency ratings, age, refrigerant cost, repair history. No pressure, no gimmicks.

Every technician on our crew is NATE-certified, background-checked, and trained to clean up after themselves. We treat your home like ours. Most of our new business comes from referrals, and we intend to keep it that way.
