How the pricing works

No big check upfront. Ever.

The build cost is wrapped into your monthly fee over 12 months, then drops to maintenance pricing. You invest in your business gradually — not all at once.

Example: Standard clients pay $150–175/month for the first 12 months, then drop to $80/month going forward. You never write a big check upfront — the build is already paid for by month 12.
